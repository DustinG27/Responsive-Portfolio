about me

I am originally from a background in film and media specializing in lighting, lighting design, and writing. I has always been my dream to work behind the camera and see how all my forvite films and shows were made. After a few years of freelancing I decided that I would rather have a stable job in one location.


This is when I made the choice to jump straight into the programming world. I have always loved technology. One of my favorite hobbies is taking apart various electronics and building them again with different shells. Once I had a grasp on hardware I wanted to move onto software and the core of computer technology.


From here, I would like to keep pursuing my hobbies and gaining more skills. Eventually, I will build a gaming cabnet from the ground up.


I also love beer. I have brewed my own as well as worked in a brewery. I got to drink on the job while I worked! How cool is that shit? Low key nickname: Hop King.
